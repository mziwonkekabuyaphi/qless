Three powerful queue modes

Choose what fits your business — or combine them

Instant Queue

First come, first serve. Customers join instantly, see their position, and get notified when it's their turn. Perfect for retail and fast-paced venues.

Live position tracking

Booking Queue

Time-slot based. Customers book a specific time, reducing wait time to zero. Ideal for clinics, consultations, and appointments.

Predictable customer flow

Hybrid Queue

Combines walk-ins + bookings with AI-powered prioritization. VIP handling, slot reservations, and dynamic adjustments in real time.
